Smart Parking Garage

One of the requirements for my degree program was completing a senior design project that encompasses everything that I learned in classes throughout the years. For two semesters, I worked in a group of four individuals where we created a system that could be implemented into present parking garages. The system we created did the following:

  • Counted the amount of vehicles in the garage.
  • Authenticated each driver that entered the garage.
  • Predicted the amount of cars that would be in the garage at certain points of the day.
